How I automated SEO for 50+ businesses with no-code AI

The challenge: digital marketing agencies needed to scale content production without proportionally scaling headcount. Manual keyword research, briefing, writing, editing, and publishing was consuming 80% of team bandwidth — leaving little room for strategy or growth.

RankForge AI solves this through a no-code architecture built on Airtable, n8n Cloud, and LLM API integrations. The system performs semantic SEO research at scale — identifying buyer-intent keyword clusters — and runs full workflow automation from brief to published article.

The result: agencies publish 12× more content per month, achieve first-page rankings within 2–4 weeks, and reduce per-article cost from ~$150 to under $2. The entire pipeline requires zero coding from end users.
